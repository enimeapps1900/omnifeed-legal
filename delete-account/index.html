How to remove your data from Omnifeed

Most of your data is already under your control

Omnifeed has no user accounts and no server that stores your content. Your connected-account tokens, drafts, and settings live only on your device. You can remove them yourself at any time:

  • Disconnect an account — open the App and go to Settings → Privacy, then disconnect any account. Its token is wiped from your device immediately.
  • Remove everything — uninstalling the App deletes all on-device data (tokens, drafts, settings).

Request deletion of diagnostic data

The only data we hold off your device is anonymous crash and usage diagnostics (see our Privacy Policy). It is not linked to your identity, but you can still ask us to delete the diagnostic data associated with your device's anonymous installation ID. Use the form below or email lflifli7@gmail.com.
